Farook International Stationery is celebrating this year their 40th Anniversary.

Farook International Stationery is celebrating this year their 35th Anniversary. Established in 1980 as a stationery retailer, it has expanded to Wholesale and then built up a huge Stationery manufacturing base in UAE.


Also, It is the Sole Agents in UAE of several international famous stationery manufacturers. Today it has 10 showrooms around UAE and one in Oman. Plus 3 factories in Dubai and Sharjah, with a total area of 250,000 sq feet. In addition, the company owns a central warehouse in Sharjah of total area of 200,000 sq feet stocking well over 18000 SKUs As well as a distribution point in Dubai.
